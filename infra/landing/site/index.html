What we are

A technology company for real-time nightlife discovery

VibeSense Tecnologia LTDA is a technology company building a mobile application for nightlife and entertainment discovery. We help users find the right venue at the right moment by combining live busyness signals, location, and detailed venue data into a single, effortless experience.

Features

What VibeSense does

⚡

Real-time busyness

See how busy a venue is right now — and how busy it usually gets — so you can pick the place that matches the night you want.

📍

Location-aware discovery

Discover bars, clubs, and entertainment venues near you, ranked by what's happening around you at this moment.

🎵

Rich venue metadata

Hours, category, vibe, and context for each place, brought together so you can decide with confidence before you go.

Company

Built in Recife, for going out anywhere

VibeSense is developed by VIBESENSE TECNOLOGIA LTDA, a technology company headquartered in Recife, Pernambuco, Brazil. We design and operate the VibeSense mobile app and the data platform that powers its real-time venue insights.
